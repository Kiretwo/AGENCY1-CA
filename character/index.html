Choose Your Race

Humans are people in stories and games. They live in towns and cities and have different jobs and dreams. Some humans like to explore and find new things. They can do many different things and help their friends on adventures. Humans are curious and want to learn about the world around them.

Elves are graceful and magical creatures in stories and games. They have pointed ears and are often very wise. Elves live in forests and love nature. They are skilled with bows and arrows and have a deep connection to magic. Elves can live for a long time and are known for their beauty and grace.

Gnomes are small, curious beings known for their love of tinkering and inventing. They live in cozy burrows underground and enjoy gardening and crafting. Gnomes are playful and have a mischievous side, often playing tricks on others. Despite their small stature, gnomes are clever and resourceful, using their intelligence to solve problems and create innovative devices.

Halflings are a small, friendly folk known for their love of food, comfort, and simple pleasures. They typically live in small villages or cozy homes nestled in peaceful countryside. Halflings are curious and adventurous, often seeking excitement beyond their quiet lives. Despite their small size, halflings are nimble and quick, making them adept at avoiding danger and getting into tight spots. They have a strong sense of community and enjoy gatherings filled with laughter and good food.

Tieflings are beings with infernal heritage, marked by distinctive features like horns, pointed tails, and sometimes red skin. This ancestry stems from a distant ancestor who made a pact with dark forces, leaving a lasting imprint on their bloodline. Tieflings often face prejudice due to their appearance, but they possess a strong will and resilience. They are known for their diverse talents, ranging from arcane magic to martial prowess, making them formidable adventurers with a hint of otherworldly charm.

Dragonborn are proud and noble creatures with the blood of dragons flowing through their veins. They have dragon-like features such as scales, sharp teeth, and the ability to breathe fire or another element. Dragonborn are known for their strength, honor, and strong sense of duty. They come from ancient dragon clans and often follow a strict code of honor. Dragonborn make natural warriors and leaders, respected for their bravery and loyalty.

Orcs are a powerful and robust humanoid race known for their fierce and aggressive nature. They are often portrayed as muscular, with prominent tusks and greenish skin. Orcs excel in combat and are skilled hunters and warriors. Despite their intimidating appearance, Orcs value strength, loyalty, and honor within their communities. They have a strong sense of kinship and often form tight-knit clans led by chieftains. Orcs are known for their resilience and determination in the face of adversity.

Choose Your class

Barbarians are strong and fierce warriors. They come from tribal backgrounds and are skilled in combat. They prefer melee weapons and light armor, relying on their endurance and rage to win battles. Barbarians have a strong connection to nature and tradition, and they can unleash powerful attacks when in battle. They are known for their toughness and ability to withstand challenges.

Warlocks are mysterious spellcasters who gain their magical powers through pacts with supernatural entities. They have access to unique and potent spells, often focusing on dark and forbidden magic. Warlocks can forge pacts with beings such as fiends, fey, or otherworldly patrons, granting them abilities beyond typical spellcasters. They are often enigmatic and have complex motivations due to their relationships with their patrons.

Sorcerers are individuals born with innate magical abilities. Unlike wizards who study magic, sorcerers possess a natural talent for spellcasting that flows through their bloodline. They can manipulate magical energy to cast spells without the need for formal training, drawing power from their unique heritage. This magical essence grants them supernatural abilities and a deep connection to arcane forces.

Rogues are agile and cunning individuals skilled in stealth and deception. They excel in tasks requiring finesse, such as picking locks and sneaking past guards. Rogues are resourceful and adapt quickly to unexpected challenges, using their wit and skills to their advantage.

Rangers are expert trackers and survivalists, at home in the wilderness. They forge deep connections with nature and are skilled archers. Rangers often serve as protectors of the wild, using their knowledge to navigate forests and mountains with ease.

Paladins are holy warriors, devoted to upholding justice and righteousness. They are skilled in combat and possess divine powers granted by their faith. Paladins use their abilities to smite evil and defend the innocent. They often serve as champions of their deity or cause, striving to bring light and order to the world.

Monks are disciplined warriors known for their mastery of unarmed combat and agility. They excel in martial arts, relying on their speed and precision to outmaneuver opponents. Monks are guided by strict codes and possess inner spiritual energy, known as ki, which they channel to perform incredible feats in battle.

Wizards are scholars of the arcane, wielding powerful magic through extensive study and practice. They possess a deep understanding of spells and can manipulate the forces of nature and reality. Wizards often carry spellbooks containing a wide range of magical knowledge, allowing them to cast spells for various purposes, from unleashing destructive forces to conjuring protective barriers.

Fighters are expert combatants skilled in the art of battle. They excel in physical combat, using various weapons and armor to protect allies and defeat enemies. Fighters come from diverse backgrounds like knights, mercenaries, and adventurers, focusing on honing their martial abilities through rigorous training. They are versatile warriors, combining strength, skill, and tactics to face any challenge.

Druids are nature's guardians, deeply connected to the wilderness. They possess magical abilities tied to animals, plants, and elemental forces. Druids can transform into animals and wield nature's magic for healing and protection. They live in harmony with nature, protecting the balance of the natural world.

Clerics are devoted to divine entities, serving as intermediaries between gods and mortals. They channel divine magic to heal allies, banish undead, and smite foes. Clerics uphold the tenets of their faith, embodying principles of compassion, justice, or zeal. They can wear armor and wield weapons, making them versatile combatants and healers.

Bards are charismatic performers and storytellers, skilled in magic and music. They weave magic through their art, inspiring allies and bewildering enemies. Bards can heal wounds with soothing melodies, cast illusions with stirring performances, or use their wit to outmaneuver adversaries. They travel the world seeking inspiration and sharing their tales, leaving a mark wherever they go.

Choose Your Background

Street Urchin:

Raised in the alleys of a bustling city, you learned to survive through wit and agility. Dodging the law and rival gangs, you scraped by through petty theft and clever cons. But now, with a taste for adventure, you seek to rise above your humble beginnings and make a name for yourself in the wider world.

Noble Heir:

Born into a life of luxury and privilege, you've always had wealth and status at your fingertips. Yet, the confines of noble society chafe against your adventurous spirit. Disillusioned with the trappings of your upbringing, you set out to forge your own destiny, seeking glory and honor beyond the gilded halls of your ancestral home.

Mercenary Veteran:

As a seasoned soldier-for-hire, you've seen your fair share of battles and bloodshed. Whether fighting for coin or cause, you've honed your skills on the frontlines of countless conflicts. But now, with war behind you, you seek a new purpose and a chance to atone for the sins of your past.

Sage Scholar:

Devoted to the pursuit of knowledge, you've spent years poring over ancient tomes and unraveling the mysteries of the arcane. Your thirst for understanding has led you to distant lands and forgotten ruins in search of lost wisdom and forbidden secrets. But beware, for some truths are better left buried.

Outlander Nomad:

Raised in the wilderness by a nomadic tribe, you've lived a life of freedom and self-reliance. Guided by the rhythms of nature, you've learned to hunt, gather, and survive in even the harshest environments. But now, drawn by curiosity or a sense of duty, you venture beyond the familiar lands of your people, eager to explore the wider world and discover what lies beyond the horizon.

Guild Artisan:

A master of your craft, you've spent years perfecting your skills as a blacksmith, jeweler, or artisan of some other trade. Whether forging weapons of war or crafting works of beauty, your hands are capable of shaping the world around you. But beneath your skillful exterior lies a burning ambition, driving you to seek new challenges and greater renown in the competitive world of commerce and craftsmanship.

Hermit Mystic:

Retreating from the chaos of civilization, you sought solitude in the remote reaches of the world. In the quiet of the wilderness or the seclusion of a hidden sanctuary, you delved deep into meditation and study, unlocking the secrets of the cosmos and the inner workings of the soul. Now, guided by visions and prophecies, you emerge from your solitude to fulfill a greater destiny.

Folk Hero:

Born among the common folk, you rose to prominence through acts of courage and selflessness. Whether defending your village from marauding monsters or standing up to tyrannical rulers, you became a beacon of hope and inspiration to those around you. But with fame comes scrutiny, and now you must navigate the complexities of heroism while staying true to your principles.

Pirate Scoundrel:

Life on the high seas is a perilous adventure, and you've embraced it with gusto. As a swashbuckling pirate, you've sailed the stormy waters, plundering treasure and evading the law with equal skill. But beneath your roguish exterior lies a code of honor, however skewed, and a thirst for freedom that drives you ever onward in search of the next daring escapade.

Haunted Outcast:

Cursed by tragedy or marked by fate, you've always felt like an outsider in the world. Whether haunted by vengeful spirits, cursed with supernatural abilities, or simply burdened by a dark past, you've learned to navigate the shadows with a wary eye and a quick blade. But as the whispers of your past grow louder, you find yourself drawn into a web of intrigue and danger that threatens to consume you.

Arcane Acolyte:

Raised in the hallowed halls of a mystical order or tutored by a reclusive mage, you've dedicated your life to the study of magic and the unraveling of its mysteries. From ancient tomes to arcane rituals, you've delved deep into the esoteric arts, mastering spells and incantations that defy comprehension. But with great power comes great responsibility, and now you must wield your magic wisely in a world where every spell has its consequences.

Criminal Mastermind:

In the underworld of crime and corruption, you've carved out a niche for yourself as a cunning schemer and ruthless opportunist. Whether running a clandestine smuggling operation or pulling off elaborate heists, you've amassed wealth and influence through sheer force of will and a knack for staying one step ahead of your rivals. But as the stakes grow higher and enemies close in, you must use all your wits and resources to maintain your grip on power.
